Voluntary diversity assessment onboarding form

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau’s (Bureau) D&I Assessment website allows financial institutions to submit D&I Assessments.

Please complete and submit this form to the Assessment Support Team to establish a point of contact (POC) for your institution. The POC will then be able to make D&I Assessment submissions to the Bureau and create additional profiles for other employees of your institution. For questions concerning this form, please contact the OMWI Regulated Entities Support Team at OMWI_regulatedentity@cfpb.gov .
